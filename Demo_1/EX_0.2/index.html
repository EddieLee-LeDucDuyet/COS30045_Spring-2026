This demo site focuses on appliance energy consumption in Australia—how to compare energy ratings, reduce standby power, and estimate running costs using kWh usage and local electricity prices.

Understanding energy use (kWh)

Appliance labels often state annual energy consumption in kWh. Lower kWh typically means lower running cost, but usage patterns matter (hours per day, brightness settings, eco modes, etc.).

  • Compare like-for-like sizes and features
  • Consider eco modes and auto-brightness
  • Turn off devices fully to reduce standby power

Quick cost estimate

Running cost ≈ kWh × price per kWh. Example: a TV using 120 kWh/year at $0.30/kWh costs about $36/year.
