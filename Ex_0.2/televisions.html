Television energy tips (AU market)

Bigger screens and higher brightness can increase energy use. Compare annual kWh, enable eco features, and avoid unnecessary standby consumption.

What affects a TV’s energy use?

  • Screen size and panel technology
  • Brightness/HDR settings
  • Usage hours per day
  • Standby and “always-on” features

Ways to reduce consumption

  • Use “Eco” / “Energy Saving” mode
  • Lower backlight/brightness
  • Enable auto power-off timers
  • Switch off at the wall when not needed
